# Coran Capshaw

## Source

- [Coran Capshaw — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coran_Capshaw)
- [Coran Capshaw, Founder, Red Light Management — Pollstar News](https://news.pollstar.com/2019/05/16/coran-capshaw-founder-red-light-management/)

## Notes

- Began managing Dave Matthews Band in 1991, having first given them a weekly gig at Trax, the Charlottesville, Virginia venue he owned.
- Founded Red Light Management in 1991 while managing Dave Matthews Band; the company grew to represent multiple artists including Phish, Chris Stapleton and The Strokes.
- Was the primary initial investor in the Bonnaroo festival, and the company holds equity positions in further large-scale festivals including Outside Lands, Lollapalooza, South by Southwest and Pilgrimage.
- The trajectory is from one personal artist relationship to a management company representing many artists, with further diversification into festival ownership.
