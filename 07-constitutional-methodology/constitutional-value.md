# Constitutional Value

## Definition

Constitutional Value is what a [Recognised Contribution](recognised-contribution.md) is worth once the system assigns it — economic, reputational, or structural worth that can be measured, paid, or built upon. It is downstream of recognition: value cannot attach to a contribution the system has not yet acknowledged, though the value itself may be realised much later, and by institutions other than the one the contribution was made within.

## Constitutional Principle

Value created within one relationship can extend beyond it — to an entire industry's terms, or to institutions and people who never directly participated in producing it. Constitutional Value is not fully accounted for by asking what a single institution captured; it must also ask what spilled over, and to whom.

## Why Value Can Outrun the Relationship That Created It

Peter Grant's 90/10 renegotiation with concert promoters reset bargaining terms for touring artists generally, not only for Led Zeppelin; Frank Barsalona's touring-circuit model is credited with improving compensation for touring musicians industry-wide, not only for Premier's own roster ([Artist Managers](managers.md); [Booking Agents & Touring Agencies](booking-agents.md); banked as [CQ-5](constitutional-analysis-method.md#cq-5-externality-value-beyond-an-institutions-own-participants)). In both cases, value produced within one institution's relationships was not contained by them.

Value can also arrive on a delay that outlasts the relationship that produced it: BMG's 2012 valuation of Mute's catalogue landed a decade after Daniel Miller had already taken the label independent again ([Independent Labels](independent-labels.md)). And value's durability is not guaranteed by its size: Hipgnosis Songs Fund acquired catalogues worth over $2bn, then was itself sold and restructured within six years — a faster cycle than any capacity-native institution studied in Research Phase 1 has shown over a comparable period, though this may reflect the underlying assets' different ages rather than a general property of capital-native value ([Publishers & Catalogue Funds](publishers.md); banked as [CQ-8](constitutional-analysis-method.md#cq-8-does-capital-native-value-have-different-durability-than-capacity-native-value)).

## Relationship to Recognised Contribution

Value is assigned to a [Recognised Contribution](recognised-contribution.md), never to a contribution that has not yet been acknowledged — recognition is the gate value passes through, not a formality that follows it.

## Relationship to Constitutional Economics

[Constitutional Economics](constitutional-economics.md) describes how Constitutional Value moves once it exists — who captures it, who it passes through, and what happens when the entity carrying a function itself becomes something that can be bought and sold.

## Examples from the Creative Ecosystem

- SoundCloud's Fan-Powered Royalties deliberately redesigned how subscriber value reaches artists, routing payment directly to the artists a given subscriber listens to rather than pooling it — reported to increase independent-creator payouts by up to 25% relative to the pro-rata model it replaced ([Streaming Platforms & DSPs](streaming-platforms.md)).
- Hipgnosis's catalogue value was realised entirely in capital terms — a $2bn+ acquisition spree, a $1.584bn resale — with no administration-service value distinguishable from the ownership position itself ([Publishers & Catalogue Funds](publishers.md)).

## Notes

The externality and durability claims above rest on evidence still Accumulating (CQ-5) or Open (CQ-8) in the [Constitutional Questions Bank](constitutional-analysis-method.md#constitutional-questions-bank) — observed patterns, not settled Constitutional Principles.
