# Dr. Dre

## Source

- [Apple Buys Dr. Dre's Beats Electronics For $3 Billion — NPR](https://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2014/05/28/316744943/apple-buys-dr-dres-beats-electronics-for-3-billion)
- [Apple to Acquire Beats Music & Beats Electronics — Apple Newsroom](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2014/05/28Apple-to-Acquire-Beats-Music-Beats-Electronics/)

## Notes

- Founded Aftermath Entertainment in 1996, a hip-hop label built around his own production work and artist development.
- Co-owned Beats (Beats Music and Beats Electronics) with Jimmy Iovine.
- Apple acquired Beats for $3 billion on May 28, 2014 ($2.6bn cash, $400m stock) — the largest acquisition in Apple's history at the time.
- Dre and Iovine each held roughly 25% equity, with pre-tax payouts of approximately $750 million each; Dre reportedly earned $620 million in 2014, the highest annual income recorded for a musician at the time.
- Dre's early public mention of the pending deal was used as leverage by Apple to reduce the price by a reported $200 million.
