# Wasserman Music / Paradigm Talent Agency

## Source

- [Paradigm Agrees to Sell Music Division to Wasserman — Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/business/business-news/paradigm-agrees-to-sell-music-division-to-wasserman-4077739/)
- [Casey Wasserman Launches Wasserman Music — Deadline](https://deadline.com/2021/04/casey-wasserman-wasserman-music-paradigm-north-american-live-music-business-1234740945/)

## Notes

- Paradigm Talent Agency had grown by acquiring a series of smaller booking agencies and artist-management boutiques, accumulating substantial debt in the process.
- The pandemic-driven shutdown of live touring left Paradigm in severe financial difficulty, forcing layoffs of over 100 staff and pay cuts, and precipitating the sale of its entire North American live-music representation business to Casey Wasserman in 2021.
- The new entity, Wasserman Music, retained Paradigm's roster — including Dave Matthews Band and Phish, both also represented by Coran Capshaw's Red Light Management, examined in the managers analysis — and expanded in 2022 by acquiring Paradigm's UK live-music business too.
- Notably, the agency owns no masters, publishing rights or other intellectual property — what was bought and sold was the representation relationships themselves: the roster and the booking business built around it.
