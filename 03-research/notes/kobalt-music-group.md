# Kobalt Music Group

## Source

- [Kobalt Music Group — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kobalt_Music_Group)

## Notes

- Founded in 2000 by Willard Ahdritz as an administrative publishing company that does not own copyrights — writers retain ownership; Kobalt collects and distributes royalties on their behalf.
- Built proprietary technology (from 2005, the Kobalt Label Services Portal) for artists and writers to track royalties directly, positioned around transparency and speed of payment rather than ownership.
- Grew to be a leading independent publisher (Independent Music Publisher of the Year, 2009 and 2010; by 2015, the top independent publisher in the UK and second overall in the US behind Sony/ATV).
- Expanded into recorded-music services via the 2011 acquisition of AWAL, then sold AWAL and Kobalt Neighbouring Rights to Sony Music Entertainment in 2021 for $430 million, refocusing on publishing and rights administration.
