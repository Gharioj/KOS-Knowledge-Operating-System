# Frank Barsalona / Premier Talent Agency

## Source

- [Frank Barsalona — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Barsalona)
- [Frank Barsalona, Music Agent Who Revolutionized Concert Business, Dead at 74 — Billboard](https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/1082948/frank-barsalona-music-agent-who-revolutionized-concert-business-dead-at-74)

## Notes

- Began as a mail-room employee at General Artists Corporation, where as a young agent he booked the first American appearances and tours for the Beatles and the Rolling Stones.
- Founded Premier Talent Agency in 1964 (sold to William Morris Agency in 2002) as the first booking agency focused specifically on rock performers, moving acts from short package-tour sets into full-length headline shows.
- Built a network of young promoters around the country to present emerging artists locally, effectively inventing the modern national concert-touring circuit as a repeatable structure, not a one-off booking.
- Credited with improving musicians' compensation and treatment so they could earn a substantial living through touring, not only record sales — an effect on industry terms generally, not only Premier's own roster (Springsteen, Led Zeppelin, Tom Petty, Van Halen, U2 among them).
- In 2005 became the only talent agent ever inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
