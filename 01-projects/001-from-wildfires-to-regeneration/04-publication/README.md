## Founding Manuscript

*Dagli Incendi alla Rigenerazione* is the original conceptual foundation of the From Wildfires to Regeneration Framework. It is retained here as a permanent historical record of the Framework's origins — it has not been superseded or deprecated, and its status is deliberately distinct from both "Published" and "Archived." It sits alongside the Version 1.0 publication suite rather than in `06-archive/`.
