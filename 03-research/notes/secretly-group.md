# Secretly Group

## Source

- [Secretly Group — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secretly_Group)
- [Numero Group Joins Secretly Canadian, Jagjaguwar, Dead Oceans — Billboard](https://www.billboard.com/music/music-news/numero-group-joins-secretly-canadian-jagjaguwar-dead-oceans-1568082/)

## Notes

- Family of independent labels based in Bloomington, Indiana: Secretly Canadian (founded 1996 by brothers Chris and Ben Swanson, Eric Weddle and Jonathan Cargill), joined by Darius Van Arman and his label Jagjaguwar in 1999, and Dead Oceans launched in 2007 by the same partners plus Phil Waldorf.
- The three labels share offices and staff in Bloomington, but maintain distinct and separate aesthetic/A&R identities per imprint.
- The Numero Group (Chicago reissue label) joined the group in 2013; the group also holds a 50% stake in Merge Records.
- Structure is founder-partnership ownership (Swansons, Van Arman, Waldorf and colleagues) with shared back-office infrastructure across imprints — not, per available public reporting, a formal artist-owned cooperative.
