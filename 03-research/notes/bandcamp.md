# Bandcamp

## Source

- [Bandcamp sold by Epic Games to Songtradr, 18 months after acquisition — DJ Mag](https://djmag.com/news/bandcamp-sold-epic-games-songtradr-18-months-after-acquisition)
- [Bandcamp Undergoes Major Layoffs After Sale — Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/business/business-news/bandcamp-layoffs-songtradr-1235619800/)

## Notes

- Founded in 2008 by Ethan Diamond as a direct-to-fan platform where artists sell digital and physical music, built around a founding policy of a high artist revenue share (commonly cited as roughly 85% to the artist on direct sales), markedly more generous than typical streaming payout economics.
- Acquired by Epic Games on March 2, 2022, with Epic stating a shared vision of "building the most open, artist-friendly ecosystem in the world."
- Sold again just 18 months later, in September 2023, to Songtradr. Of 118 Bandcamp staff, roughly 60 were offered positions at Songtradr; the rest were laid off.
- Songtradr said it would continue operating Bandcamp with an "artist-first revenue share," but declined to confirm whether artist revenue shares, user experience or the Bandcamp Daily editorial platform would be affected by the change in ownership.
