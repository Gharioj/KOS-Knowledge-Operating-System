# Creative Artists Agency (CAA) Music Division

## Source

- [Creative Artists Agency's Music Department: 35 Years of Igniting Stars — Billboard](https://www.billboard.com/music/music-news/creative-artists-agency-music-dept-8493020/)
- [Creative Artists Agency — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Creative_Artists_Agency)

## Notes

- CAA's music division launched in 1984 under founder Tom Ross, quickly working with Rick Springfield, the band America, and Prince ahead of *Purple Rain*.
- Through the 1980s, CAA's music division fine-tuned and scaled the national-touring model Frank Barsalona had established at Premier Talent in the 1960s–70s; opened a Nashville office in 1991 to expand into country music.
- Structured around an integrated, cross-divisional culture — music agents collaborate with agents in film, brand partnerships, publishing and licensing, rather than working in silos — attributed by longtime music-division head Rob Light as central to the agency's success.
- CAA's Global Touring division now coordinates negotiations, bookings and worldwide logistics for many of the industry's highest-grossing tours.
